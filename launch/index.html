About Vulcan-1

We built the rocket to fly the engine

Jia Yang
The Vulcan-1 rocket is designed by the SEDS UCSD team in an attempt to be the first student team in the world to successfully fly and recover a rocket powered by a 3D printed engine.
Vulcan-1 is powered by our 2nd 3D-printed rocket engine. Fueled by LOx and RP-1, the engine is capable of producing 750lbs of thrust.
