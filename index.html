About SEDS UCSD

SEDS UCSD, or Students for the Exploration and Development of Space at the University of California, San Diego, is a collection of motivated, passionate students determined to complement our studies with meaningful projects.

Current Projects

Colossus

Colossus is a newly designed static fire test stand, providing valuable data. Designed to be built on a trailer, it will be extremely mobile, eliminating hours of prep spent on previous systems.

Learn more...

Aurora

Aurora is an autonomous, rocket-powered lander powered by four engines. The lander will use artificial intelligence to navigate clear of hazards and execute flight maneuvers to land at a safe location.

Learn more...

Propulsion

SEDS UCSD has been known for its groundbreaking, 3D-printed engines. These include Triteia's Callan, the Vulcan engine, and a newly, currently being designed, LOx/Methane engine.
