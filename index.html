About SEDS UCSD

SEDS UCSD, or Students for the Exploration and Development of Space at the University of California, San Diego, is a collection of motivated, passionate students determined to complement our studies with meaningful projects.

Triteia Cubesat

A 6U satellite designed for NASA's CubeQuest competition and to be launched on the new Space Launch System in 2018

Colossus SFS

Colossus is a newly designed Static Fire Test stand. Designed to be built on a trailer, it will be extremely mobile, eliminating hours of prep spent on previous systems.
