About Vulcan-II

The Vulcan-II rocket is currently being designed by the SEDS UCSD team.

The rocket will be powered by the second iteration of our Vulcan engine. Optimized for a higher thrust and pressure, the Vulcan-II engine will launch a newly designed rocket to a target altitude of 45,000 ft.

VULCAN-II

Powered by Ignus-II
