About Colossus

Colossus is a newly designed Static Fire Test stand. Designed to be built on a trailer, it will be extremely mobile, eliminating hours of prep spent on previous systems.

NASA engineers have graciously provided us with invaluable resources and advice for redesigning the system. Our end goal is to create a flexible system that will be able to accommodate engines of various sizes and thrusts, allowing other organizations to utilize Colossus for testing.

Tech Specs

Structure designed to withstand 5000lbf of thrust
Current plumbing sizing configuration allows for 2700lbs
Maximum pressure of 1350psi
Maximum mass flow rate of 4gal/s
Cryogenic compatible on both the LOx and propellant side
