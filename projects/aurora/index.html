Aurora

An autonomous, rocket-powered lander

aurora is dedicated to the research and development of a landing module for cargo transportation in space.

We strive to develop a reusable landing module capable of landing a payload accurately and reliably to the surface of moon or Mars.

The proposed concept is a 4 engine robotic lander to maximize payload size and enable easy ‘drop-n-go’ payload delivery. The lander will use artificial intelligence to navigate clear of hazards and execute flight maneuvers to land at a safe location.
